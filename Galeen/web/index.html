Why ICBT Study Abroad?

we are an unit of ICBT Campus to provide guidance, consultation and placement services to students for overseas. ICBT study Abroad staff provides professional and Quality advice to help you find the best option based on your academic and financial requirements.


Our staff offers advice and assistance in

● Consultation and assistance on selecting the best and accurate study option
● Secure placement at a suitable university to meet the required qualification and finances.
● Preparing Application and Visa documentations
● Assist students in the entire Visa application and process.
● Pre-departure briefing.
● Arranging accommodation.
● Organize Meet & great events with Sri Lanka students on campus.

ALL OUR SERVICE ARE ABSOLUTELY FREE!!!
